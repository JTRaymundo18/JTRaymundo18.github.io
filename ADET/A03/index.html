ISTEEM ANNOUNCEMENTS

Valve Teases Steam Deck 2 for 2027

Valve has hinted at a successor to the Steam Deck, tentatively referred to as the Steam Deck 2. The company is awaiting advancements in chip technology to provide a significant performance boost before moving forward with development .

Lenovo Legion Go S Launches with SteamOS

The Lenovo Legion Go S, a handheld gaming device featuring SteamOS, is set to release on May 25. Priced at $549.99, it boasts an 8-inch OLED display, 500GB storage, and 16GB RAM, with a premium model offering 1TB storage and 32GB RAM for $749. Despite a $50 price increase, it remains more affordable than Windows-based alternatives.

Steam's 2025 Sales Calendar Revealed

Steam's 2025 sales calendar has been announced, featuring major events like the Summer Sale from June 26 to July 10, and the Winter Sale from December 17 to January 5. Other events include Wargames Fest, Creature Collector Fest, and Steam Next Fest, offering gamers various opportunities to purchase games at discounted prices.

Steam Implementing 12% VAT in PH Starting June 2025

Filipino gamers should prepare for a price increase on Steam purchases, as the platform will begin charging a 12% value-added tax (VAT) on all digital transactions in the Philippines starting June 1, 2025. This change follows the enactment of Republic Act No. 12023, which imposes a 12% VAT on digital services provided by both local and foreign companies.

Microsoft's Potential Integration of Steam into Xbox Ecosystem

Microsoft is reportedly moving towards integrating Steam into its Xbox ecosystem. An image briefly published by Microsoft showed a new Xbox interface featuring a filter for Steam within a user's game library, suggesting a potential partnership between Microsoft and Valve to unify experiences across Windows and Xbox platforms.

GAME ANNOUNCEMENTS

InZoi

Step into the role of a divine overseer in InZoi, a hyper-realistic life simulation game where you shape the lives of your citizens in a modern, vibrant city. Guide their careers, relationships, and daily routines as their unseen guardian, influencing everything from the weather to social events. Every decision you make ripples through the lives of your citizens, crafting a unique urban narrative.

Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2

Set amidst the chaos of 15th-century Bohemia, Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2 continues the journey of Henry, the humble blacksmith’s son turned warrior. With the kingdom torn apart by civil war, Henry must navigate a treacherous world of politics, betrayal, and battlefield glory to fulfill his personal quest for justice and identity. The line between loyalty and vengeance blurs as alliances shift and kingdoms fall.

The Wolf Among Us 2

In The Wolf Among Us 2, Bigby Wolf returns as sheriff of Fabletown, drawn into a chilling investigation when Fables begin mysteriously disappearing during a harsh New York winter. As he uncovers a web of secrets linking the magical underworld to the mundane city, Bigby must confront his own past and the fragile trust he's built. With tensions rising and danger closing in, every choice he makes could reshape the fate of Fabletown.

Project 007

Uncover the origins of the world’s most iconic spy in Project 007. As a newly recruited agent in the British Secret Service, you'll complete covert missions, unravel an international conspiracy, and earn your 00 status. Set in a gritty, cinematic world, this original Bond narrative explores the cost of loyalty, the burden of secrets, and the making of a legend.

Little Nightmares 3

In Little Nightmares III, two new protagonists, Low and Alone, must escape The Spiral—a haunting and distorted world filled with monstrous residents and shifting realities. Navigating twisted towns, forgotten fairgrounds, and terrifying dreamscapes, they must work together to survive. But in a place where fear takes physical form, not even their bond may be enough to escape the nightmare.
