EYE OF THE STORM

ASTROGGLES

  • It requires 2 Moon Shards and 1 Potato.
  • Blueprint is given by a holographic transmission during Moon Storms.
  • A suitable headgear during sandstorms & Moonstorms.

MOONGLEAM

  • Randomly form and float inside Moonstorms, & deals 10 damage per crackle.
  • Can be captured using Bug Nets.
  • A major requirement in spawning the Celestial Boss.

MISSHAPEN BIRD

  • It attacks any player that gets close by lobbing a projectile in an arc.
  • Land in flocks alongside Moonblind Crows inside of Moonstorms.
  • Can be captured using a Bird Trap, & can be placed inside a Birdcage.

MOONBLIND CROW

  • It attacks any player that gets close by biting them.
  • Land in flocks alongside Misshapen Birds inside of Moonstorms.
  • Can be captured using a Bird Trap, & can be placed inside a Birdcage.

GRAINY TRANSMISSION

  • He can be found in the Moonstormthat spawn after the Mysterious Energy is created.
  • He will periodically ask for one of five tools, which will spawn around him during the containment process.
  • Winona and WX-78 are closely related to him in some unfortunate circumstances that dragged both of them to the Constant.

LUNAR SIPHONATOR

  • Its crafting recipe is given by the Grainy Transmission upon arriving inside the Moonstorm.
  • Rains down meteors upon completing the whole construction of the Incomplete Experiment.
  • It calls the Champion of the Lunar Island.
